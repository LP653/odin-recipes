Brownies

Description

Based on the Allrecipes Brick oven pizza brooklyn style recipe

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tbls extra-virgin olive oil

Topping

  • 6 oz low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbls extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn

Instructions

  1. Make the dought:
    1. Pour warm water in large bowl.
    2. Sprinkle yeast over top.
    3. Let stand for 5 minutes.
    4. Stir in cold water and salt.
    5. Stir in flour, 1 cup at a time until incorporated.
  2. Knead dough on a floured surface until smooth (~10 min)
  3. Divide in half and form into two tight dough balls.
  4. Coat with olive oil and refrigerate in a sealed container for >= 16 hrs
  5. Make pizzas (Remove from fridge one hour prior to use)
