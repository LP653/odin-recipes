Brownies

Description

Based on the Allrecipes Best Brownies recipe

Ingredients

Brownie

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Frosting

  • 3 tbls butter, softened
  • 3 tbls unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbls honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F (175 deg C)
  2. Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan
  3. Melt 1/2 cup butter in large saucepan.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 tsp vanilla.
  5. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  6. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan (~25-30 min).
  8. Make chocolate frosting: Combine softened butter, confectioners' sugar, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, and 1 tsp vanilla extract in a bowl. Stir until smooth
  9. Let brownies briefly cool before frosting but frost while they are still warm.
